About Us

Miss Patina’s design concept is to create individual clothing with elements of vintage glamour, whilst focusing on intricate details, such as lace or mesh overlays, brocade and embroidery. Inspirations for design are taken from previous iconic fashions and fused with today’s catwalk trends. Inspired by vintage and a love for art, design and culture, there are many styles that Miss Patina caters to.
